Frequently Ask Questions

Frequently asked questions regarding Maint Event TEDxUNDIP 3.0

What is REVEAL THE SUN?

Started with TEDxUndip in 2019 which affiliated with BEM FEB Undip, now TEDxUNDIP 2.0 is back with a fully independent background. TEDxUNDIP 2.0 designed to help our communities to spark conversation and connection through TED experiences at the university level. Local, National, and Global ideas come to the center of Java to share ideas worth spreading.

What can i get from this event?

By participating in REVEAL THE SUN through Presale 2 phase, you will get these benefits below : 1 ticket Main Event, E-certificate, Networking, Knowledge, Amazing talks from inspirational speakers, Semarang Exposure, Special e-booklet from TEDxUNDIP, E-voucher discount from Bakso Abang Kita 5.000, Sei Sapi Kitong 25%, McD (16K for 1pc Ayam McD + Reg. Rice, 10K for Iced Coffee, 14K for Reg. Coca-cola + Reg. French Fries), Emina 15% and Idiomology 15% for language course.

How can I attend the TEDx event?

The event is ticketed and thus anyone with an entry ticket will be able to attend the event. Presale 2 tickets are currently open for sale from February 20 2020 until the quota exceeds. A limited number of seats will be available and the tickets will be sold online on the TICKETING section of TEDxUNDIP website. The price will be mentioned in the registration window. You can choose either bank transfer or e-wallet as payment methods for buying the ticket.

How can I know when the ticket sales begin?

All of the information regarding ticket sales will be informed through TEDxUNDIP Instagram (ini di link ke instagram.com/tedxundip aja). Turn on your instagram notification to stay updated!

What is the last date for registration?

There is no definite date since the window will be open until the maximum allowed seats are filled. The counter on the registration page will show the number of seats left. However, to ensure a seat, it is advisable to book the seat as soon as possible. Presale 2 is currently open, so grab your ticket as soon as possible because we will close the presale when the quota already exceeds.

What is the schedule of the event?

Reveal The Sun is set to be scheduled virtually on Sunday, March 14 2020 at (UTC+7) 02.00 PM - 05 PM through TEDxUNDIP website livestream. The Livestreaming Access will be sent to your email three days before the event takes place.
