Rules A

Rules B

Andreas Darwis Triada

He is a famous Indonesian photographer who was born in Solo and his photographs have the characteristics of human interest and landscape. He has won many awards including: Gold award from Matsushita Award Japan and Anugerah Fotografi Indonesia (AFI). By 2002 he created his own photography class, those of you who enjoy photography might want to join his class and even register for our TEDxUNDIP 3.0 photography competition!

Adi Putra

Adi Putra is photographer from Indonesia. Adi is well-known photographer who specializes in surrealist, cinematic, and nostalgic styles. Even though a pandemic happened, Adi Putra is stille quite active in the art world, showing and selling his crations at the Macan Museum in Jakarta (which pretty sure, if some of you are from Jabodetabek, you would probably know this exhibition :D).
